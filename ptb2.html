Podcast Logo Case Study

  • Client
    Client: Plead the Belly
    Date: April 2018

    This Logo was designed for the true crime podcast called "Plead the Belly". This podcast explores the nuances of women in crime.

    Client Website
  • Process
    Understand:

    The first step was getting to know needs and wants of PTB. I needed to not only meet their specific requests but get into what inspired their podcast, and what stories they were going to tell as to create a better and more useable design.

    Research:

    The second stage led me to look into the "true crime" genre and what other podcasts were doing with their logos.

    Sketch:

    The third stage was sketching out several directions and imagery that could be used to represent the podcast.

    Design:

    After many sketch interactions, I was able to narrow in on what PTB was liking at a potential logo.

    Implement:

    After putting the final design into Adobe Illustrator we went back and forth several times between these next two stages. I would send over a version and then wait for feedback for changes.

    Evaluate:

    Finally the logo was ready to be placed on their website.
